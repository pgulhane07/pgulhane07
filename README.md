### 📌 About Me
I am a **Software Engineer, AWS Certified Developer Associate and AI Researcher** with over 3 years of experience in the Fintech sector, specializing in secure, scalable backend microservices and applied artificial intelligence. I recently completed my **M.S. in Computer Science and Engineering** at the University at Buffalo. My recent academic research, published at [IEEE CogMI](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11417044), focuses on advanced medical AI, specifically architecting a MedVLM+RAG pipeline using SimCLR and Grad-CAM to achieve high diagnostic accuracy for neurological conditions. I am passionate about bridging the gap between robust AWS infrastructure and cutting-edge Agentic AI workflows.

- **Cloud & Infrastructure:** AWS (Certified Developer Associate), Azure, Kubernetes, Terraform, Docker
